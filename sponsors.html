Sponsors

We strive to make WSUCon a completely free event to all of our participants. Without the hard work of the Linux Users' Group and the generous donations of all of our sponsors, food, prizes, and other awesome goodies just wouldn't be possible.


Our event comes at a considerable cost, we are asking for your help through sponsoring the event! There are many ways to sponsor WSUCon, you could donate:

  • Your time that can be used to help with the planning and execution of WSUCon.
  • Prizes for tournaments, raffles, etc...
  • A meal for WSUCon so we don't starve during this amazing 24-hour event.
  • Money that can go to any one of these various options (and equipment) depending on the needs of the Con.

Supporter: $50

This sponsorship level comes with a small logo on our website.

Bronze: $500

This sponsorship level comes with a small logo on both our website and posters.

Silver: $1,000

This sponsorship level comes with a bigger logo on our posters, fliers, and website.

Gold: $2,000+

This sponsorship level comes with a large logo on our posters, fliers, website, and shirts!

As a sponsor, you would be allowing an amazing event to continue to be free and open to all. If you are interested, please feel free to contact us.
