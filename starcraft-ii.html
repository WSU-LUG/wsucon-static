Starcraft II - May 02nd @ 1pm

The Tournament Rules

  1. You must be registered and present for the tournament.
  2. The tournament is restricted to Heart of the Swarm ONLY. Should Call of the Void somehow be released before the tournament begins a vote will be held to see if the tournament should be played with that expansion instead. A 2/3rds majority will be necessary to change the expansion being played.
  3. The map pool is as follows:
    • Alterzim Stronghold TE
    • Daedalus Point
    • Frost LE
    • Habitation Station LE
    • Heavy Rain LE
    • Polar Night LE
    • Yeonsu LE
  4. The map pool is subject to change if exploits are discovered or the maps are removed from competitive play for any reason.
  5. First map pick is determined by rock-paper-scissors, following map are chosen by the loser of the previous match.
  6. The tournament starts promptly at 1pm.
  7. After playing a round of 3 with your tournament opponent you will report your results to the tournament organizer and then wait for your next opponent to finish their match.
  8. There is a 15 minute grace period that begins after both you and your opponent become ready (finish their previous games), if you are unable to play by the end of the grace period you will be immediately disqualified and your opponent will progress to the next round.
  9. If necessary and both players agree, breaks can be taken in between individual matches to get food, go to the bathroom, etc.
